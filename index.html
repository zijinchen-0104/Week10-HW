Australia's Property Evaluation

This page shows the distribution of Melbourne property and the evaluation of Australian property.

Melbourne property distribution

From the right stacked bar chart, we can know the distribution of Melbourne properties is concentrated near the city and diverges along the coastline. The saturation red color shows that the higher priced properties are near the city and along the sea. The north and east area's properties are relatively lower.

Australian property price VS property type among each city

From the left stacked bar chart, we can know that Sydney's properties have the highest price among all Australian cities, the second one is Melbourne. The number annotations on the chart show the average properties price in each city. It also shows that the house prices are highest and the unit prices are lowest, no matter in which city.
